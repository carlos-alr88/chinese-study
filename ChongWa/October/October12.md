Grammar
Question Pronoun (QPr)

QPr followed by 都 to say "all" or "none"

1. S + QPr + (N) + 都 + V. 
   我 + 什么 +青菜 + 都 + 吃 = I eat all kinds of vegetables
2. QPr + 都 + 不 + V + O
    谁 + 都 + 不 + 想做 + 饭
